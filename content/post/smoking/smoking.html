From 2012 to 2019 the Scottish Survey Core Question(SSCQ), which is a project drawing together multiple household survey(for more in about the SSCQ click here ), was used as a indicator for people smoking in Scotland.

And what you will find when looking at the data that people decide to quit the cigarrete. The causation can not be told from the data but we can discover the sometimes extreme difference and drop of for each age group of the population and each consticuency in Scotland
